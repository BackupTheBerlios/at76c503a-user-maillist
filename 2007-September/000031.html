>  Is there any official repository holding the most recent
> driver version?

It's hard to say.  It was the CVS repository, but then it moved to a
decentralized repository.  It has no official status, but the plan is to
move development to the kernel.  Unfortunately, the driver is required
to use mac80211 to go to the kernel, and this hasn't been done yet.

The driver can be found at http://git.80211libre.org/at76_usb.git

It can also be found in the wireless-dev repository, but it's out of
date there:
git://git.kernel.org/pub/scm/linux/kernel/git/linville/wireless-dev.git
